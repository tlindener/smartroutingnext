Smart Routing Next

Smart Routing Next is a next generation route planning app. It uses personalized preferences and external data services like Deutsche Bahn Call a Bike or openweathermap to find the best route for you!

Credits

Smart Routing Next was created during the forth DB Hackathon in Berlin by Steffen Baumann, Tobias Lindener and Michael Siebert.
